Practice and Exam Questions

Note that the Practice Question(s) do not confine to the exam spec exactly but rather test knowledge on the subject.

  1. An onion cell is shown in a textbook with a width of 2cm. Given that the onion cell’s real size is 0.25mm, what is the magnification of the image?
  2. Name two advantages of an electron microscope compared to a standard light microscope. You should aim to make at least two points.
  3. A cell of width 340μm is magnified by 100 times. What is the image size, in cm?
  4. When observing a slide under a microscope, first the ____ focus must be used.
  5. Why is the smallest objective lens used first when observing something with a microscope?
