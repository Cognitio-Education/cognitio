Digestion and the Digestive System

Revision Notes

The role of the digestive system is to digest, or to break down food, and to absorb their nutrients for other bodily functions. For this absorption to happen, the food must be small and soluble, thus why digestion occurs.

Types of Digestion

There are two types of digestion used to break down food:

  • Mechanical digestion: Physical breaking down or churning of food.

  • Chemical digestion: The use of special chemicals called enzymes in order to break down the food chemically.

Different organs in the digestive system have different ways of digesting food.
